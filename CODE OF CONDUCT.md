# B# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in B# a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Regardless of:

- Experience level
- Gender identity
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Religion
- Nationality

We are committed to maintaining a healthy community.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Provide helpful feedback
- Accept criticism gracefully
- Focus on technical merit
- Encourage collaboration

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing private information without permission

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to:

- Remove inappropriate comments
- Close harmful issues
- Reject pull requests violating standards
- Ban repeat offenders

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

Please contact the maintainer directly through the repository.

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## Commitment

B# aims to be a welcoming environment for learners, contributors, and professionals alike.

Let’s build responsibly.
